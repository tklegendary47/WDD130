Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Splash & Raft as a website is to promote white water rafting activities.

It provides professional guides, buying or renting rafting equipment, allows individuals to book up for rafting trips and so much more.

This website will be able to support white water rafting as a sport hence being a towerlight for tourists and people who are interested in the sport especially on holidays.

This will benefit the company in terms of revenue. A portion of the income made is used to support a local orphanage.

Audience

Are you looking for the ultimate white water rafting experience ?

Splash & Raft is your number 1 place for professional rafting guides, buying or renting rafting equipment and booking up for rafting trips. Families out on holiday brace yourselves as you visit the Victoria Falls and Zambezi River.

Adrenaline, ecstacy and experience are the only words that go with the current while you are on this exciting adventure. Have fun!
