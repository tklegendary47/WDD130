FAQS

Your questions are important to us.

Welcome to Frequently Asked Questions. Here we answer the most asked questions from users around the globe. Mount Motivation is a growing community and so we are here to review your wants and needs. Your questions are a sign of how much appreciation you have for our work. Through you we are able to improve and become better.

One of the most asked questions we have had so far is 'Will Mount Motivation introduce more features?'. The answer is YES! Our team is already working on introducing new features so that the site will be more user-friendly than ever before. We want users to be able to communicate and share inspiring ideas hence growing the community.

How Can I Share Motivational Images too?

Through the sign-up and Join button, one can become a member of the Mount Motivation community. When they have signed up, they gain access to features such as creating a profile and posting content.

What does the site aim to achieve?

From movies to series, series to anime; every creator out there is trying to convey a message. These messages can offer lifelong lessons that can inspire the lowest underdog into becoming the champion of tomorrow. Many successful people started off at rock bottom. They got inspired by a particular scene from an anime or movie which sparked enthusiasm and optimism within them to achieve greatness. We as Mount Motivation aim to do the same for you. We aim to inspire and bring a smile on your face, letting you know that life is not so bad when you see it from a different point of view. A view that can be only experienced from the top of the mountain; on Mount Motivation :)
