Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Mount Motivation, the peak of inspiration. The website was designed and created with sole purpose to motivate.

With vast inspirational content from movies, anime and especially Biblical scriptures we offer the necessary motivation to bring your hope adrenaline back in higher gear.

We aim to inspire each and every individual from all walks of life hence uplifting them to reaching goals physically and spiritually.

Audience

Are you wandering in despair? Are you having a bad day or simply want something to inspire you to become better than yesterday?

Rest assured, have a little more faith and despair no more! You've reached the top of Mount Motivation so look unto the horizon and have hope once more.
